# 📚 Comprehensive English Quiz: A1-A2 Level

---

**1. Every morning, David _______ to God.**
A) pray
B) prays
C) praying
D) prayed

*Grammar Point:* Present Simple (habits/routines). 3rd person singular adds -s.
*Explanation:* "Every morning" = habit. "David" = he/she/it, so verb needs -s.
*Japanese:* 「毎朝」=習慣。三人称単数は-sが必要。

**Answer: B**

---

**2. Yesterday, the disciples _______ across the lake.**
A) sail
B) sails
C) sailing
D) sailed

*Grammar Point:* Past Simple (completed actions). Regular verbs add -ed.
*Explanation:* "Yesterday" = finished past time. Regular past tense = sailed.
*Japanese:* 「昨日」=過去の完了。「ed」で規則的な過去形。

**Answer: D**

---

**3. She _______ in the garden now.**
A) work
B) works
C) is working
D) worked

*Grammar Point:* Present Continuous (happening now).
*Explanation:* "Now" = at this moment. Form: am/is/are + verb-ing.
*Japanese:* 「今」=進行形。be動詞 + -ing。

**Answer: C**

---

**4. Moses _______ the people through the desert many years ago.**
A) lead
B) leads
C) leading
D) led

*Grammar Point:* Past Simple - Irregular verbs.
*Explanation:* "Many years ago" = past. "Lead" → "led" (irregular, not leaded).
*Japanese:* 不規則動詞の過去形。lead → led。

**Answer: D**

---

**5. The sun _______ in the east every day.**
A) rise
B) rises
C) is rising
D) rised

*Grammar Point:* Present Simple (universal truths/facts).
*Explanation:* Universal truth = always true, use present simple. "Sun" = it, so -es.
*Japanese:* 普遍的真実は現在形。三人称なので-es。

**Answer: B**

---

**6. Last week, they _______ bread and fish to the crowd.**
A) give
B) gives
C) gave
D) given

*Grammar Point:* Past Simple - Irregular verbs.
*Explanation:* "Last week" = past. "Give" → "gave" (irregular).
*Japanese:* 不規則動詞 give → gave。

**Answer: C**

---

**7. Right now, the children _______ songs of praise.**
A) sing
B) sings
C) are singing
D) sang

*Grammar Point:* Present Continuous (action in progress).
*Explanation:* "Right now" = at this moment. Form: are + singing.
*Japanese:* 「今まさに」=進行形。are singing。

**Answer: C**

---

**8. Paul usually _______ letters to the churches.**
A) write
B) writes
C) writing
D) wrote

*Grammar Point:* Present Simple with frequency adverbs.
*Explanation:* "Usually" = habit/routine. "Paul" = he, so -s.
*Japanese:* 「通常」=習慣。三人称単数は-s。

**Answer: B**

---

**9. Two thousand years ago, Jesus _______ in Bethlehem.**
A) born
B) is born
C) was born
D) were born

*Grammar Point:* Past Simple Passive.
*Explanation:* "Two thousand years ago" = past. Passive: was/were + past participle. "Jesus" = was.
*Japanese:* 受動態の過去形。was born = 生まれた。

**Answer: C**

---

**10. Martha _______ in the kitchen while Mary listened to Jesus.**
A) work
B) works
C) was working
D) worked

*Grammar Point:* Past Continuous (background action).
*Explanation:* "While" = two actions happening together. Background action = was working.
*Japanese:* 過去進行形。while = 〜している間に。

**Answer: C**

*Explanation:* "The Bible" = specific, unique book (only one).

**12. He wants to be _______ good shepherd.**
A) a

D) no article

*Explanation:* "A good shepherd" = one example of many, not specific.
*Japanese:* 不特定の「一つの」には a/an。good は子音なので a。

**Answer: A**

**13. _______ sun is shining today.**
A) A
B) An
C) The
D) No article

*Explanation:* "The sun" = only one sun exists. Always "the".

**14. They ate _______ apple from the tree.**
A) a

D) no article

*Grammar Point:* Indefinite article before vowel sounds.
*Explanation:* "An" before vowel sounds (a-e-i-o-u). Apple starts with 'a' sound.

**Answer: B**

**15. He is _______ honest man.**
A) a
B) an
C) the
D) no article

*Grammar Point:* Article choice by sound, not spelling.
*Explanation:* "Honest" starts with vowel SOUND (silent h: 'onest'), so "an".
*Japanese:* スペルではなく音で判断。h が無音なので an。

**Answer: B**

**16. _______ God created the world.**
A) A
B) An
C) The
D) No article

*Grammar Point:* No article for proper names (God as name).
*Explanation:* "God" used as name = no article. (Compare: "the god of war" = specific type).
*Japanese:* 固有名詞（名前）には冠詞なし。

**Answer: D**

**17. She found _______ coin that was lost.**

**18. He has _______ faith in God.**
